   Our Vision
 


          Digital expresso aims to tell stories through our daily cup of coffee. We want to bring out the best in our customers and open their eyes to new
         experiences with each cup of our coffee. We encourage our customers to also tell their own stories with each cup request we do.
 
  
 
   Our Mission
 


          Each of our coffee beans are made from the harvests of local products that help support farmers all over the country. Our baristas strive to be create
         the best possible coffee experience that our customers may receive. Both our coffe beans and its process tell its own stories in its own way per cup
         once received by our customers. We want to encourage our own customers as well to submit and request any kind of coffee type they want to our local
         baristas to allow each customer to share their own story to the world.
